## Identified Gaps → Suggested Topics

### Tier 1 — High SEO value, product-driving

**1. Thruster vs Quad vs Twin Fin: Which Setup is Right for You?**
- Keywords: "thruster vs quad fins", "surfboard fin setup guide" (~high volume)
- Why: Missing despite selling thruster sets; top GEO query ("what fin setup should I use?"); promotes all surf fin SKUs
- Products: FCS/Futures thruster sets, side fins, knubster

**2. What is a Knubster Fin? How the Centre Fin Changes Your Wave**
- Keywords: "knubster fin", "2+1 fin setup", "what does a centre fin do"
- Why: Very specific product question Eisbach Riders can own; they sell the FCS/Futures knubsters; GEO goldmine (AI asked this constantly)
- Products: FCS Centre Knubster, Futures Centre Knubster

**3. SUP Fin Size Guide: Which Length is Right for Your Board?**
- Keywords: "SUP fin size chart", "what size fin for SUP board"
- Why: #24 covers general selection; a size-specific guide with a table/chart ranks separately and drives the specific fin products
- Products: Classic (short), Touring (medium), Race (long)

**4. Race Fins for SUP: Do You Actually Need One?**
- Keywords: "SUP race fin", "racing SUP fin guide"
- Why: The Race Fin is the highest-priced SUP SKU (€59.95); no article targets buyers considering the upgrade
- Products: Race Fin US Box

**5. GNARWALL Review: Wall-Mounting Your Surfboard at Home**
- Keywords: "surfboard wall mount", "surfboard wall rack review", "how to hang surfboard"
- Why: Article #9 is a general storage guide; a product-focused GNARWALL review ranks for purchase-intent queries
- Products: all GNARWALL SKUs

## GEO Writing Principles (apply to every article)

- Open with a **direct answer** in the first paragraph (AI pulls lede)
- Use **H2 questions** as section headers (e.g. "What's the difference between a thruster and a quad?")
- Include a **FAQ section** at the bottom with 3–5 concise Q&A pairs
- State specific numbers, measurements, product names clearly
- Avoid vague filler — AI models deprioritize low-density content
- End with internal CTA to the relevant collection
